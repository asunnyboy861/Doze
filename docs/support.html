Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Download Doze from the App Store and tap "Log Sleep" to record your first session. No account needed.

Do I need an Apple Watch?

No. Doze uses manual logging. You simply enter when you went to sleep and when you woke up. No wearable device required.

How do I edit a sleep record?

Go to the History tab, tap on any session, and tap "Edit" to modify the times, mood, or notes.

Does Doze sync with Apple Health?

Yes. When you enable HealthKit, your sleep records are automatically saved to Apple Health.

Subscription FAQ

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find Doze and tap it
  5. Choose "Cancel Subscription" or change your plan

Or visit: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open Doze, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases".

What's the difference between Monthly and Yearly?

Both plans offer the same features. The Yearly plan offers a 37% discount compared to paying monthly.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can continue using premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll revert to the free version with 30-day history.

I purchased Lifetime but it's not showing

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your Lifetime access.
