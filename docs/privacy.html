Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Information We Collect

Doze collects minimal data necessary to provide the service:

  • Sleep Records: Start time, end time, mood, and notes you voluntarily enter.
  • HealthKit Data: Sleep analysis data shared with Apple Health when you enable HealthKit sync.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the Doze sleep tracking service. We do not:

  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Collect analytics beyond basic crash reporting
  • Require an account or personal information

Data Storage

All sleep data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable iCloud sync (Premium feature), data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit with end-to-end encryption.

Third-Party Services

Doze integrates with Apple HealthKit for sleep data synchronization. HealthKit data remains under your control through Apple's privacy settings.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual sleep records at any time within the app. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Doze is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
