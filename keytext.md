## Description

Doze is the simplest way to track your sleep. No wearables, no complex setup — just tap and log.

WHY DOZE?

Most sleep apps want to be your sleep coach. Doze just wants to be your sleep diary. No scores, no judgments, no guilt — just a simple record of when you slept and how you felt.

QUICK LOGGING
Record your sleep in 3 seconds. Tap "Log Sleep," set your bedtime and wake time, and you're done. That's it.

MOOD TRACKING
How did you sleep? Choose from five simple moods: Terrible, Bad, Okay, Good, or Great. No complicated questionnaires.

WEEKLY & MONTHLY TRENDS
See your sleep patterns at a glance with beautiful charts. Spot trends, find your best nights, and understand your rhythm.

APPLE HEALTH INTEGRATION
Sync your sleep data with Apple Health. All your health information in one place.

ICLOUD SYNC (PREMIUM)
Keep your sleep data in sync across all your Apple devices with iCloud.

DATA EXPORT (PREMIUM)
Export your sleep history as CSV for personal analysis or to share with your doctor.
