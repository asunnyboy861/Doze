Most sleep apps want to be your sleep coach. Doze just wants to be your sleep diary. No scores, no judgments, no guilt - just a simple record of when you slept and how you felt.

Record your sleep in 3 seconds. Tap Log Sleep, set your bedtime and wake time, and you are done. That is it.

How did you sleep? Choose from five simple moods: Terrible, Bad, Okay, Good, or Great. No complicated questionnaires.
